# YouTube Script Templates

## Template 1: Tutorial/How-To Script

```
[HOOK - 0:00-0:15]
"If you've ever struggled with [problem], this video will change everything. In the next [time], I'll show you exactly how to [achieve result]."

[CREDIBILITY - 0:15-0:30]
"I've spent [time period] perfecting this method, and it's helped me [specific achievement]. Now I'm sharing the exact system with you."

[ROADMAP - 0:30-0:45]
"We'll cover:
1. [Main point 1]
2. [Main point 2]
3. [Main point 3]
Plus a bonus tip at the end that most people miss."

[MAIN CONTENT - 0:45-X:XX]
"Let's start with [Point 1]..."
- Explain concept
- Show example
- Common mistake to avoid
- Quick win tip

"Now for [Point 2]..."
[Repeat structure]

[CONCLUSION - Final 30 seconds]
"So to recap: [summarize 3 main points]
If you found this helpful, [call to action]
And that bonus tip I mentioned? [deliver bonus]"
```

## Template 2: Story-Driven Educational Script

```
[COLD OPEN - 0:00-0:10]
[Start with most dramatic moment]
"And that's when everything went wrong..."

[REWIND - 0:10-0:30]
"Let me back up. Three weeks ago, I decided to [undertake challenge]. What I discovered changed how I think about [topic]."

[SETUP - 0:30-2:00]
- Context and background
- Why this matters
- What's at stake
- The plan

[JOURNEY - 2:00-8:00]
- Chronological progression
- Obstacles encountered
- Lessons learned along the way
- Building tension toward climax

[CLIMAX - 8:00-9:00]
- The big moment
- Success or failure
- Emotional peak

[RESOLUTION - 9:00-10:00]
- What was learned
- How viewers can apply this
- Broader implications
- Tease next video
```

## Template 3: Challenge/Experiment Script

```
[INSTANT HOOK - 0:00-0:05]
"I'm going to attempt [seemingly impossible thing]."

[RULES SETUP - 0:05-0:30]
"Here's how this works:
- Rule 1: [specific constraint]
- Rule 2: [another constraint]
- If I fail: [consequence]
- If I succeed: [reward]"

[PREPARATION - 0:30-2:00]
- Gathering materials/team
- Explaining the science/logic
- Predictions and expectations
- Building anticipation

[ATTEMPT 1 - 2:00-4:00]
- First try
- What went wrong
- Lessons learned
- Adjustments needed

[ATTEMPT 2 - 4:00-6:00]
- Implementing changes
- New challenges arise
- Tension building

[FINAL ATTEMPT - 6:00-9:00]
- All or nothing
- Maximum tension
- Success or failure
- Immediate reaction

[WRAP-UP - 9:00-10:00]
- What we learned
- Could you do this?
- Thank supporters/team
- Next challenge teaser
```

## Template 4: Comparison/Review Script

```
[HOOK - 0:00-0:15]
"Everyone says [Product A] is the best, but after testing [number] options, the winner surprised even me."

[CRITERIA SETUP - 0:15-0:45]
"I tested based on:
1. [Criterion 1] (most important)
2. [Criterion 2]
3. [Criterion 3]
4. [Criterion 4]
5. Real-world usage"

[TESTING MONTAGE - 0:45-1:30]
- Quick shots of all products
- Preview interesting moments
- Build curiosity

[DETAILED TESTING - 1:30-7:00]
For each product:
- First impressions
- Test results for each criterion
- Pros and cons
- Price consideration
- Who it's best for

[RANKINGS - 7:00-8:30]
- Counting down from worst to best
- Explaining placements
- Surprise revelations

[WINNER & CONCLUSION - 8:30-10:00]
- Why this one wins
- Best value option
- Best premium option
- Personal recommendation
- Where to buy/links
```

## Quick Tips for All Templates

### Opening Lines That Hook
- "This changes everything..."
- "I was completely wrong about..."
- "Nobody talks about this, but..."
- "After [time period] of research..."
- "The industry doesn't want you to know..."

### Transition Phrases
- "But here's where it gets interesting..."
- "Now, you might be thinking..."
- "This is when things took a turn..."
- "But wait, there's more to this story..."
- "Here's the part nobody expects..."

### Closing Calls-to-Action
- "Try this and let me know your results"
- "What's your experience with [topic]?"
- "Should I do a part 2?"
- "Download my free guide below"
- "Join the discussion in the comments"

### Retention Techniques Throughout
- Promise reveals: "In a moment, I'll show you..."
- Callbacks: "Remember earlier when..."
- Foreshadowing: "This becomes important later"
- Mini-cliffhangers: "But first, we need to understand..."
- Pattern interrupts: Change pace/energy suddenly
